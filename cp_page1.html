Welcome to My Site!

Thank you for stopping by my website. My name is Christian Peterson - I'm a Northeastern University student studying Data Science & Business. In the past, I've always been obsessed with learning about different industries and how technology intersects with them. It's funny because, ever since I was young, I used to love watching construction sites, or something like raw material mining, and I used to ask for model water towers and factories (for my train set) for Christmas, instead of light-up shoes or something like that. On here, I will go into depth about some emerging industries, or subsets of current/matured ones, and also reference new technologies in those industries. The world is ever-changing but, currently, the development of technologies like AI and Quantum Computing will not only transform the tech industries, among others, but will also require a build out of infrastructure (think energy and such) to support it's growth and continued operation. To give this website more of a purpose, I will go into further depth about trends that will be transformed within the coming years, and how they might look after this transformation. I can't predict the future, but at a high level the information will be sufficient.
